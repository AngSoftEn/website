About Me

Hi, I'm Angelos! I'm 28 years old and from Greece, passionate about powering the future with solar energy. My journey in this sector has been incredibly rewarding, covering the entire lifecycle of solar park development

I've built my career on hands-on installation and construction, and I've successfully managed complex cabling projects totaling over 30MW. Additionally, I have experience in modifying and installing communication networks for seamless system telemetry, as well as installing automation systems and overseeing tracker systems and their sensors.

For the past 3 years, I've been ensuring the smooth operation of 300+ parks, leveraging my expertise in troubleshooting and maintenance to maximize their performance and reliability. I'm dedicated to promoting the efficiency and sustainability of solar energy solutions, and I believe in making solar energy reliable and effective for everyone.
